Research notes

For the first iteration of the prototype we focused on the most straightforward scenario to test the agent taking a claim over the phone.

We found that agents adapted well to the flow of the claim as well as the style of content designed to read out to a citizen. There were some issues where agents:

  • did not realise when a claim was found after entering a National Insurance number
  • misinterpreted the security question answers
  • anticipated the living and working abroad pages
  • were uncomfortable with the declaration
  • got confused about ‘other names’ when taking details of a spouse
  • More information on the award page or further details about the claimant, such as benefit information to inform payment frequency, would have been useful
