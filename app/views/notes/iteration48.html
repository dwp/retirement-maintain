Scenarios

  • Agent needs to mark an account as deferred when a claimant calls up and decides to defer their pension after they have made a claim

Business requirements

  • If claimant request comes in before SPa, the date of deferral is always SPa
  • If claimant request comes in after SPa, the date of deferral can either be ‘now’ (i.e. the start of the benefit week in which the request was made) or any date up to 28 days in the future
