Award

Award history

Current award:
An award is a benefit weekly entitlement amount, made up of several components. The current award is the award currently in payment.

Previous awards:
Awards can change based on circumstances, all previous awards are recorded in award history.

Award details:
Previous awards component breakdowns can be seen in award details. Award details also shows any adjustments or withheld amounts.

Adjustments

Current adjustment:
An adjustment is applied to a payment calculation of award entitlement. The adjustment does not change the award entitlement amount however the citizen will receive a lower payment until the adjustment period has ended.
For example, a debt adjustment would be applied to an award for a period of time until the debt has been recovered.
Adjustments can be applied and viewed in the awards area of the service. Adjustments are also shown in payment details.

Upcoming adjustment or update:
An upcoming adjustment or update is when there is a planned adjustment or change of award that has been scheduled for a future date.
For example, we can calculate uprating before it is applied to the award based on the current award.

Previous adjustments:
How a previous adjustment shows depends on if the award it is applied to is in payment or if it is a previous award. Adjustments can span over multiple awards, so the same adjustment may be viewable from multiple award details. If the adjustment was applied to the current award it will show in previous adjustments reveal, if it was applied to a previous award, you will see "yes" in the adjustment colulm as well as seeing the adjustment details in the award details.
